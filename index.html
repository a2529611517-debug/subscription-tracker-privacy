Privacy Policy for Subscription Tracker

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Subscription Tracker (the "Extension") does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. All data processing occurs locally on the user's device.

When you authorize the Extension to access your Gmail account, it only searches for emails containing keywords such as "subscription", "renewal", or "receipt". The Extension does not read other emails. The results are stored locally using Chrome's storage API and are never uploaded.

You can revoke the Extension's access at any time via your Google Account permissions page.
